About Kyoto

Kyoto is a beatutiful city situated in the western part of Japan. It has many temples and shrines as it was the religious center of Japan for a long time. It was also the capital of Japan for more than one thousand years. But, Kyoto is not just a city of the past as it is still a major center culturally.

Scope

  • New developments in software process, metrics, formal models, CSCW, object-oriented technology, analysis and testing, requirements engineering, reengineering, etc.
  • The scope will be expanded to include other software desciplines, such as networking, multi-media, database, natural language, etc.
