W. Morven Gentleman heads the Software Engineering Laboratory in the Institute for Information Technology at the National Research Council of Canada. He has taught at the University of Waterloo and has been among the technical staff at Bell Telephone Laboratories. His Ph.D. from Princeton in 1966 is in Mathematics. His research activities include software engineering, computer architecture, robotics, computer algebra, and numerical analysis.
