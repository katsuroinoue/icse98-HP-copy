Registration

The ICSE 97 Advance Program, which contains conference registration materials, will be mailed in January, 1997 to members of ACM/SIGSOFT, IEEE Computer Society TCSE, and cooperating societies. To request additional copies of the Advance Program, contact the ICSE 97 Conference Office.
