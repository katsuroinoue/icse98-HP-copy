ICSE Bibliography

A bibliography of articles published in the Proceedings of past ICSEs are available on the Web. All articles from ICSE1 (1975) through ICSE17 (1995) are referenced. The references are given in BibTeX format.

The references are part of a comprehensive collection of computer science bibliographies that is mirrored on sites around the world.
