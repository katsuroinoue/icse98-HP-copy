CONFERENCE PROCEEDINGS FORMATS

  • Latex style file(icse98.sty)

    • Hint for icse98.sty:
    1. In the "\author" part of your paper you should put "\\" right after "\end{center} }" where you want to break a line. For example, if the number of authors is 4 then put "\\" at the end of the definition of the second author. Note that you cannot put white line in the "\author" part.
    2. If you have more than 3 authors then you have to modify icse98.sty; seach the sentence "*** CHECK HERE ***" in icse98.sty and follow the instruction written there.
