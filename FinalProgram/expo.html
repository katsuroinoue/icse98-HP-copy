ICSE98 EXPO

Tuesday, April 21 - Friday, April 24

4/21-23(09:00~17:00), 4/24(09:00~16:00)

The Exhibit offers commercial exhibit space to vendors as well as research exhibit space for products by academic and government research laboratories. There are two types of exhibits.

- Commercial products from vendors.

- Research products from academia, industrial research laboratories, and government laboratories.

Some exhibit space remains, and applications will be accepted on a first-come first-served basis.

Contact: Shinichi Honiden (icseexpo@po.iijnet.or.jp)
